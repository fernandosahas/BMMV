About Us

A Legacy of Excellence in Education

For nearly 150 years, Badulla National School has been at the forefront of educational excellence in Sri Lanka. We combine traditional values with modern teaching methodologies to create well-rounded individuals.

Our commitment to academic excellence, character development, and community service has made us a beacon of hope and inspiration for generations. We believe in nurturing not just scholars, but responsible citizens who will lead our nation into the future.

Our History

Founded in 1875

Established in 1875, Badulla Central College has served as a beacon of knowledge and character for nearly 150 years. From humble beginnings, our school has grown into a leading educational institution in the Uva Province, renowned for its academic achievements, cultural heritage, and commitment to community service. We cherish our legacy and continue to inspire generations of learners.

Our School

Explore Our School Sections

We nurture students at every stage of their educational journey, from Grade 6 to Grade 13, with dedicated sections and specialized programs.

Grades 6 - 7

Our Junior Section provides a strong foundation in core subjects, focusing on curiosity, creativity, and essential skills. Students are encouraged to explore, ask questions, and develop a love for learning in a supportive environment.

  • Interactive Classrooms
  • Language & Science Labs
  • Co-curricular Activities
Learn More

Grades 8 - 11

The Middle Section prepares students for national examinations, offering a broad curriculum and guidance for subject selection. We emphasize analytical thinking, teamwork, and leadership through academic and extracurricular programs.

  • O/L Exam Preparation
  • Clubs & Societies
  • Sports & Arts
Learn More

Grades 12 - 13

Our Senior Section offers Advanced Level streams in Science, Commerce, and Arts. Students receive expert guidance, career counseling, and opportunities for leadership, preparing them for university and beyond.

  • A/L Streams: Science, Commerce, Arts
  • University Entrance Support
  • Leadership & Community Service
